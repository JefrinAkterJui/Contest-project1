Rules of the Game

In this game, you will play as three players: Player 1, Player 2, and Player 3. Player 1 starts by selecting a number. Players 2 and 3 will have five chances to guess the correct number. If Player 2 successfully guesses the number within their five attempts, Player 3 will not have another chance to think. We hope you enjoy our game and wish you the best of luck!
